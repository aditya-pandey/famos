The Wellbeing Gap

Academic success is inextricably linked to emotional stability. Today’s students face unprecedented pressures, yet schools often operate in a vacuum, disconnected from the primary support system: the family. When home life is fragmented, student wellbeing declines, leading to decreased engagement, higher absenteeism, and behavioral challenges that hinder the entire classroom.
